World Climate Gradients

Searching for Climate Patterns at the Equator

The following weather data was compiled from over 500 cities around the world to highlight global weather patterns over various latitudinal ranges and what this reveals about conditions at the equator. It takes an approach of comparing 4 key variables to the distances from the equator:

  •  Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude

  •  Humidity (%) vs. Latitude

  •  Temperature (F) vs. Latitude

  •  Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude
